Geometry Dash is a very Fun, Geometerically & Musically Educational, and Creative game(at least to me). Its geometric component includes vectors, gravity(including gravity-shift), constant velocity toward the "+x component" movement(it's the horizontal side of the gravity), jump, wave, ufo forces which can controlled with manual controlles, etc. There are various kinds of creepy, fun, synthesic(?) music included in the game, and also, you can create your own level and share & play other's level.
